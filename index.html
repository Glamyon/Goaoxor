FAQ

What kind of platform are you?
A creative project matching platform, matching top Chinese film, animation, short video teams, providing full process guarantees.
How to handle language communication?
Mainly in English, provide Chinese-English translation when necessary, confirm language preferences and time zone arrangements.
How to ensure fund security?
Use third-party escrow platforms (e.g., Alipay International, Payoneer), contracts clearly define fund release conditions.
Can projects be expedited?
Expedited available, feasibility assessed during matching, expedited fees explained (usually +20-50%).
Who owns the intellectual property?
After project completion, intellectual property belongs to the client, including source files, contracts clearly state copyright ownership.
